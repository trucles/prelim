Class Pricing Information

Flying Trapeze Classes
Individual Class: $75
4-Class Pack: $200

Valid for any scheduled flying trapeze classes.

All purchases are non-transferable and are valid for 3 months from the date of purchase.
Non-Flying Trapeze Aerial Classes
Individual Class: $30
4-Class Pack: $100

Valid for any scheduled aerial silks, aerial hammock, lyra, static trapeze, or aerial rope classes.

All purchases are non-transferable and are valid for 3 months from the date of purchase.
Conditioning Classes
Individual Class: $20
4-Class Pack: $75

Valid for any scheduled conditioning or flexibility/stretching classes.

All purchases are non-transferable and are valid for 3 months from the date of purchase.
Open Gym Access Package
Monthly Membership: $60

Allows for individual training time outside of scheduled classes. Staff will be available for assistance, but no formal instruction will be provided. Use of flying trapeze rig is not included. Certification must be provided for any non-NSAA-provided equipment and approval must be obtained from a general manager prior to use. Open Gym Access can be purchased through the Prepaid packages section of Bookeo.
Premium Gym Membership
Monthly Membership: $80

This monthly membership includes Open Gym Access as well as a 40% discount on non-flying trapeze aerial classes, 30% discount on flying trapeze classes, and 20% discount on conditioning classes. Discount calculated based on individual class prices. Premium Gym Membership can be purchased through the Membership section of Bookeo.
Private Lessons
Individual Class: $70 for a 1-hour session for 1 student
Additional Students: $30 for each additional student up to 6

Valid for instruction in aerial silks, aerial hammock, lyra, static trapeze, or aerial rope.
