Open Gym
Open Gym is generally available between 9am-9pm daily when space is available. Please check Bookeo schedule for exact availability. Due to COVID restrictions, the following limitations will be in place temporarily:

  1. Due to need to limit building occupancy, Open Gym will not be available when other classes/events are in session.
  2. Due to need to rest fabric equipment for 48 hours between use, Open Gym using NSAA-provided silks/hammocks will only be available on Fridays (hammock) and Saturdays (silks) on a limited basis. Open Gym using participant-provided equipment will be available daily.
